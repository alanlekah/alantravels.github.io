Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Who We Are

This website is operated by Alan Lekah, doing business as DJ Travels Global, a division of Global Exception LLC. Our business address is 401 S Audubon Ave, Tampa, FL 33609. You can reach us at alan@cruisesbyalan.com or 630-561-0344.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a quote request or contact form (name, email, phone number, travel preferences)
  • Use our Cruise Booking Audit tool (cruise line, sail date, payment status, booking details)
  • Subscribe to our email newsletter (email address)
  • Contact us via phone, email, or WhatsApp

We also automatically collect certain technical information through Google Analytics, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring website. Google Ads may collect click identifiers (gclid) when you arrive via a paid advertisement.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your cruise quote requests and provide personalized travel recommendations
  • Process and manage cruise bookings on your behalf
  • Send you cruise deals and travel tips if you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Improve our website and advertising through analytics
  • Track advertising performance to optimize our marketing spend

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Cruise lines and travel suppliers as necessary to process your bookings
  • Service providers that help us operate our website (Google Analytics, formsubmit.co for form processing)
  • Google Ads for advertising measurement and optimization

Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data. Google Ads may also set cookies to measure ad performance. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Quote requests and booking information are retained for the duration of your travel planning and for a reasonable period afterward for follow-up and record-keeping.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at alan@cruisesbyalan.com. If you wish to unsubscribe from our newsletter, you can do so by contacting us directly.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
