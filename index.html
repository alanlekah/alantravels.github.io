Frequently Asked Questions

How does booking with a cruise travel agent work?

It's simple! Tell me about your dream vacation — dates, budget, destinations, and preferences. I'll research the best options, present you with personalized recommendations, and handle all the booking details. You get expert guidance from start to finish, completely free.

Is your service really free? How do you get paid?

Yes, my service is 100% free to you! I earn a commission directly from the cruise lines when you book. The price you pay is the same (or often better) than booking directly, but you get my expertise and personalized service at no extra cost.

Which cruise lines do you work with?

I work with all major cruise lines including Royal Caribbean, Celebrity Cruises, Norwegian Cruise Line, Carnival, Princess Cruises, MSC Cruises, Virgin Voyages, and many more. Whatever your style or budget, I can find the perfect match.

I've never been on a cruise before. Is it right for me?

Absolutely! Cruises are perfect for first-timers because almost everything is included — dining, entertainment, accommodation, and transportation between destinations. I specialize in helping first-time cruisers find the ideal ship and itinerary for their interests.

Can you help me book shore excursions?

Yes! I can help you find amazing shore excursions through trusted partners like Viator, often at better prices than booking through the cruise line directly. From snorkeling in the Caribbean to wine tasting in the Mediterranean, I'll help you make the most of every port.

What's included in a typical cruise fare?

Most cruise fares include your stateroom, all meals at main dining venues and buffets, entertainment shows, pool and fitness center access, and kids' clubs. Extras like specialty dining, spa treatments, shore excursions, and drink packages are additional but I can help you find the best deals on those too.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by cruise line and fare type. Many cruise lines offer flexible cancellation up to a certain number of days before sailing. I always explain the specific cancellation terms before you book, and I can help you choose options with the most flexibility if that's important to you.

Can you help with group cruises or special events?

Absolutely! Group cruises are one of my specialties. Whether it's a family reunion, wedding, corporate retreat, or friends getaway, I can coordinate group bookings with special rates, private events, and dedicated group amenities. Groups of 8 or more staterooms often qualify for extra perks.
