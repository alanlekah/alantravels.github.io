How My Insider Deals Work

Yes. I have access to "Consortium Rates" and "Group Space" that allow me to offer lower prices or extra onboard credits that the general public can't access. I also stack multiple promotions to get you the lowest possible net price.

What happens if the price drops after I book?

This is where I save you the most money. I monitor prices daily. If the rate drops before your final payment date, I'll contact the cruise line and get your booking repriced automatically. You save money while doing zero work.
